
In the Pitches file, the column headers are:
1.	Vendor ID
2.	Vendor name
3.	Year and Week
4.	Number of vegan hotdogs
5.	Number of meat hotdogs
6.	Onions (Kg)
7.	Ketchup (Litres)
The validation rules for each field are:
Field	Validation
Vendor ID	Must be upper case
Must be two letters, an underscore, and three digits
Vendor name	Must be between 2 and 25 characters long
Year and Week	Must be the format of YYYYWW
WW must be between 1 and 52, inclusive
Number of vegan hotdogs	Must be divisible by 10
Number of meat hotdogs	Must be divisible by 10
Onions (Kg)	Must be a decimal number, in half kilogram increments
Ketchup (Litres)	Must be an integer between 1 and 4
